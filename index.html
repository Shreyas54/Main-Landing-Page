Praise’s

It is powered by a 1,000W electric motor while the Praise’s 72V, 45Ah lithium-ion battery provides a claimed range of 170-200km The Praise is offered with three speed modes Eco (top speed limited to 35kph), Sporty (65kph) and Turbo (75kph).The seat height stands at 774mm, making it easier to get on and off, while the ground clearance is ample at 170mm.
